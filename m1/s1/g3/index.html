Foccaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cramberriese, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Souce

Ingredients

    For the foccaccia
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups luke warm water
  • 1/4 cup extravirgin oil, plus more for the pan
    For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tublespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen Cranberry

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium sizedmixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with the paddle attachment , mix toghether all
    the dought ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the
    dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand incorporate the last of the flour...
